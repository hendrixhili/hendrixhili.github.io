About

I work at the seam between communication systems and the hardware that carries them — coming from RF and electromagnetics, and moving toward the circuits and photonics underneath.

I have a strong foundation in RF/electromagnetics and communication systems, and I am now transitioning toward RFIC design and integrated photonics. My hands-on work spans millimeter-wave simulation, antenna and metasurface design, and software-defined radio.

I am building circuit- and photonic-level foundations to bridge system- and EM-level intuition with integrated circuit and photonic design — the place where my communications background meets the device physics I want to master next.
